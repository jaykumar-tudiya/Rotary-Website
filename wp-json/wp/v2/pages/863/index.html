ROTARY COMMUNITY CORPS<\/div><\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

As per the norms prevailing in the Rotary International, A Rotary Community Corps is a group of non-Rotarians who share our commitment to service. RCC members plan and carry out projects in their communities and support local Rotary club projects.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

There are more than 7,500 corps in over 80 countries.\u00a0Rotary Club of Deonar has five such RCCs \u2013 villages that have been adopted and over the last 15 years we have installed over 86 handpumps, 55+ toilet block, 35+Borewells and have supported the health initiatives by conducting medical camps, cataract surgeries and supplied senior citizens with walking sticks.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
