The objects of Rotary is to encourage and foster the ideal of service as a basis of worthy enterprise and, in particular, to encourage and foster:<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n\n

The development of acquaintance as an opportunity for service;<\/div>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n
High ethical standards in business and professions; the recognition of the worthiness of all useful occupations; and the dignifying of each Rotarian's occupation as an opportunity to serve society;<\/div>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n
The application of the ideal of service in each Rotarian's personal, business and community life;<\/div>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n
The advancement of international understanding, goodwill, and peace through a world fellowship of business and professional persons united in the ideal of service.<\/div>
<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

Rotary Motto\u00a0:\u00a0Service Above Self\u00a0<\/strong><\/p>\n\n\n

AVENUES OF SERVICE<\/div><\/div><\/div>\n\n\n\n

(From Rotary Internation Web Site)<\/a>\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Rotary\u2019s commitment to Service Above Self has been channeled through the five\u00a0Avenues of Service, which form the foundation of club activity.\u00a0<\/p>\n\n\n

Club Service<\/div>
Club Service focuses on strengthening fellowship and ensuring the smooth functioning of Rotary clubs. Learn about effective club service in Membership and Training.<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n
Vocational Service<\/div>
Vocational Service involves club members serving others through their professions and aspiring to high ethical standards. Rotarians, as business leaders, share skills and expertise through their vocations, and they inspire others in the process.<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n
Community Service<\/div>
Community Service is the opportunity Rotary clubs have to implement club projects and activities that improve life in the local community. Learn more about community service and assessing your community.<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n
International Service<\/div>
International Service encompasses efforts to expand Rotary\u2019s humanitarian reach around the world and to promote world understanding and peace. It includes everything from contributing to PolioPlus to helping Rotary Youth Exchange students adjust to their host countries.<\/div><\/div>\n\n\n
New Generations <\/div>
The Avenue of New Generations recognizes the positive change implemented by youth and young adults involved in leadership development activities, community and international service projects, and exchange programs that enrich and foster world peace and cultural understanding.<\/div><\/div>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
