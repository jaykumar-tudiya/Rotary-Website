ROTARACT CLUB OF DEONAR<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Rotaract Club of Deonar came into existence on the 16th of June, 1989 under the Presidency of Rtr. Dnyanesh Amonkar when Rtn. Kulbhusha Jetly was the president of Rotary Club of Deonar. A group of 17 young and enthusiastic youngsters started on a journey to thrive for excellence. The meeting place suggested was at the heart of one of the best residential colonies in Bombay surrounded by hillocks (Anushakti Nagar). <\/p>\n\n\n\n

Year after year the club has maintained its strength and we are proud to have some of our best members leading the club as Presidents. Today we at the Rotaract Club of Deonar, are about 60 Rotaractors, from Anushaktinagar, Chembur, Ghatkopar, Worli, Mulund, Matunga and New Mumbai. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

We have a excellent mix of working professionals (HP, CRISIL, HEWITT, ZEE TV, ABN AMRO, MBT, PCS) & budding professionals (from institutes like JJ, Rizvi, St.Xaviers,Somaiya, SIES, Welingkars, etc).The professional expertise includes Marketing, HR, Medicine, Finance, Law, Software, Engineering, Travel. Some of our past members are currently pursuing higher studies abroad or are on their peak of careers in different parts of the world, specifically the USA and the UK.Over the years our club has generated a large number of members into The Responsible Youth.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
